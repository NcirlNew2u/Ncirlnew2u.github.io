Our Goal

Here at New2U, we want to try and reduce our carbon footprint on this planet. We aim to try and find new homes for no longer wanted appliances, furniture, and jewellery. Rehoming these items is the most efficient method of recycling them as there is no manufacturing processes involved.

At New2U all items seen on site are for free and simply just a passing on of materials from one person to another. We hope that the availability of functioning second-hand products for zero cost will encourage people to try our online store before going out and purchasing a new product that will have some sort emissions trail attached to it.

If you have unwanted items, please get in contact with us and we can upload them to our site and try and find them a new home. Product Condition

As the products on this site are second hand, each item will be in a different condition to the next. Each item sent into us is reviewed by our team and if we believe it has a condition rating from A to D then we will post it here on the site. If we think your particular product is in a condition lower than this, we will direct you to the most appropriate place to recycle your particular item.
